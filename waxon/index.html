    Working with kids and youth

    Association of Citizens "Neki Novi Klinci" is voluntarily, musical, independant, non- governmental, multiethnic and nonprofitable Association established for achieving common interests of members of Association.

    Encouraged with lack of vision and attention to culture in my town, Association is established in 2014 on my initiative. Goal of this Association is to provide non-formal music education for chlidren and youth in my and surounding towns and to encourage them to expand ther acquaintances. Since 2014 to today, more than 500 students have passed through this Association learning how to play guitar, keyboard, drums, bass guitar and violin. While leading this Association I gained experience in writing projects and implementing them (organizing concerts, enrollments for new members, camps and etc.), working with documentation, working with large and small groups of children and youth.
